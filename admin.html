Manage your portfolio items using Google Sheets. Because this portfolio is integrated dynamically, any changes you make to the sheet will automatically and instantly appear on your website!

Step 1: Create or Edit Your Google Sheet

  1. Go to sheets.new
  2. Ensure you have these EXACT column headers in Row 1:
idtitlecategorycategoryDisplaydescriptionresultimage
1Gaming StreamthumbnailYouTube ThumbnailHigh-impact gaming...40% CTRhttps://example.com/img.png

Step 2: Publish Your Sheet

  1. In Google Sheets, click File → Share → Publish to web
  2. Under "Link", select: Entire Document and Comma-separated values (.csv)
  3. Click Publish and copy the generated link. It will look like this:
https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/e/2PACX-1vXXXXX/pub?output=csv

Step 3: Update script.js

  1. If you ever create a NEW sheet, quickly open script.js on your website codebase.
  2. Replace the GOOGLE_SHEET_URL constant variable at the very top.
  3. Deploy to Netlify and you're good to go!
You're all set! As long as your sheet is published and columns match, everything is synced automatically.
Note: The category column should strictly be 'thumbnail', 'promotion', or 'social' to match the gallery filters.
